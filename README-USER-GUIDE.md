# 🏆 OmniSports Tournament Manager — Operational User Guide

Welcome to the **OmniSports Tournament Manager**! This guide is designed for sports tournament organizers, event managers, coordinators, and sponsors. It explains how to utilize our digital platform without any complex technical jargon.

Our goal is to make planning, scheduling, registering, and tracking your sports competitions as seamless and engaging as possible.

---

## 🎯 What is OmniSports?

OmniSports is a unified control center for tournament administration. It allows you to create events, enroll teams, set match schedules, record scores, and notify all active users of live updates as they happen on the field.

---

## 🎮 Key Core Features

### 1. Unified Event Center
*   **Create Tournaments**: Set up tournaments for multiple sports categories (Cricket, Badminton, Soccer, Basketball, and Tennis) with specified arenas/venues, dates, times, and team limits.
*   **Event Status Grid**: Instantly see which sports events are in progress, fully booked, or scheduled for a later date.

### 2. Team Registration & Roster Workspace
*   **Register Contenders**: Seamlessly sign up clubs or teams into sport-specific events.
*   **Manage Capacity**: Easily track the remaining register spots. The system automatically updates slots and locks entries when the tournament is full, avoiding double-bookings.

### 3. Interactive Calendars & Scoring Desk
*   **Dynamic Match Schedules**: Display a timetable of matches, showing dates, times, competing teams, and current progress.
*   **Scorekeeper Panel**: Record scores of ongoing matches. Once saved, scores are published to the entire network.

### 4. Real-time Live Score Transmissions
*   **No Refreshes Needed**: Thanks to our unified smart pipeline, whenever a score changes or a new event is published, an elegant scoreboard notification card slides onto the screen of every active user.
*   **Quick Jumps**: Click **"Go to Schedule Tab"** directly from an alert card to inspect the corresponding bracket.

### 5. Corporate Sponsorship Board
*   **Financial Tracking**: Manage sponsorship tiers (Gold, Silver, Bronze) and keep track of total financial backing.
*   **Brand Placement**: Oversee active sponsors supporting each sport event to ensure sponsor visibility matches contract obligations.

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Operator Instructions

### Scenario A: Registering a New Tournament
1.  Navigate to the left sidebar menu and click **Events**.
2.  Press the **Create New Event** button.
3.  Fill in the simple parameters:
    *   *Sport Name* (e.g. "Summer Bash Soccer")
    *   *Sport Type* (Pick from dropdown)
    *   *Venue* (e.g. "Centennial Arena")
    *   *Maximum Teams* (Set upper boundary)
4.  Click **Submit Event**. All managers logged in elsewhere will receive an instantaneous notification that a new sport event is open for enrollment!

### Scenario B: Recording Match Scores
1.  Navigate to the **Schedule** tab on the left.
2.  Find the active match you wish to score.
3.  Click **Update Score**.
4.  Key in the current scores for Team A and Team B, select the status (e.g., *Ongoing* or *Completed*), and save.
5.  A **Live Score Updated** scoreboard pop-up will slide into screen for all spectators on the network, displaying updated team badges and scoreboards automatically.

## 💡 Troubleshooting FAQs

❓ **Do I need to refresh my browser to see updated scores?**
> **No!** The platform receives live broadcasts automatically. If a scorekeeper alters a score, your screen updates the boards and displays a floating slide-in toaster with the fresh scores instantly.

❓ **Can teams sign up when a tournament is full?**
> **No.** The platform locks registrations automatically once the "Maximum Teams" constraint is met.

❓ **Are active managers alerted across different computers?**
> **Yes.** Because the server is linked to a live streaming station, changes made on one computer flash on everyone else's screen in less than a second. Keep an eye on the bottom right of your screen for real-time flashes!
